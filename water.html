Interpretation caveats

Manaus 17.7 m is an operational surcharge trigger, not a universal navigation-stop threshold. Porto Velho 4.0 m is a navigation-regulation reference, but the station-datum interpretation must be displayed before using it as a station event cutoff. Barcarena is not assigned zero shock merely because it lacks a local usable gauge.
